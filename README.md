# 510(k) Decoded — Practitioner Templates

**Free, open-source regulatory affairs templates for FDA 510(k) premarket notification submissions.**

Part of the *510(k) Decoded* practitioner case study series — a body of peer-reviewed-standard regulatory affairs analysis designed to support practitioners navigating FDA medical device clearance.

---

## What's in this repository

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `510k_Interactive_Templates_Sonawane.pdf` | Fillable PDF — three practitioner templates with live checkboxes, dropdowns, and text fields |

### Templates included in the PDF

**Template I — Predicate Selection Checklist**
A four-phase structured checklist for selecting and validating a 510(k) predicate device: regulatory eligibility screening, intended use analysis, technological characteristics comparison, and strategic defensibility assessment.

**Template II — Substantial Equivalence Comparison Template**
A structured comparison table for documenting the SE determination under 21 CFR §807.100(b): intended use comparison, technological characteristics comparison with same/different dropdowns, and conclusion fields.

**Template III — 510(k) Submission Readiness Checklist**
An eight-section pre-submission quality review checklist covering: device description, predicate and SE argument, performance testing, biocompatibility, software and cybersecurity, labeling, quality system, and submission package completeness.

## How to use

1. Download `510k_Interactive_Templates_Sonawane.pdf`
2. Open in **Adobe Acrobat Reader** (free) — interactive fields require Acrobat, not a browser PDF viewer
3. Fill in fields directly: checkboxes, dropdowns, and text fields are all active
4. Save a completed copy for each submission you are working on

## Regulatory basis

All templates are grounded in:

- **21 U.S.C. §360(k)** — 510(k) premarket notification statutory requirement
- **21 CFR Part 807, Subpart E** — 510(k) procedural regulations
- **21 CFR §807.100(b)** — Substantial equivalence determination standard
- **FDA Guidance:** *The 510(k) Program: Evaluating Substantial Equivalence in Premarket Notifications* (July 2014)
- **FDA Guidance:** *Content of Premarket Submissions for Device Software Functions* (June 2023)
- **FDA Guidance:** *Cybersecurity in Medical Devices: Quality System Considerations and Content of Premarket Submissions* (September 2023)
- **ISO 10993-1:2018** — Biological evaluation of medical devices
